WELDER




Welder is a 1-year regular ITI course offered by Industrial Training Institute to train the students to work at the industrial level. If you are interested in welding metal or plastic parts then you can join the ITI welder course. The students are provided the knowledge about all the welding techniques, different types of metals, AC and DC machines, introduction and use of safety equipment during welding, gas regulator etc.

Your future scope in welder trade


  • As Industrialization is booming in India. It creates a lot of job opportunities. The number of factories is increasing day by day and it also creates a demand for a welder in the market. So it has a good scope in the future. You can work in any factory as a Welder man. ITI Electrician Course Details
  • Indian government’s goal of “power for all” owing to which the power sector is poised for significant expansion.
  • You can become a self-employed by opening welding service provider shop, welding service eqipments shop.
  • There are a lot of options to make a career in government organizations such as, Railways, ECIL
  • Positive investment climate, expansion of machine industry.
  • Government electricity board, Municipal Corporations, Hydropower plants, Thermal power plants, Nuclear power plants etc.
