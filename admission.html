Apply For Admission

1- Fill Online Application Form

Students must fill the online application form with complete details that has been asked in the form. It is the first and most important step towards taking admission in ncvtmis portal.

2- Review and Submit the Application

Once, you have reviewed the application form, finish it by submitting it on the NCVT website and get ready for the next step. Any mistake can be easily rectified in this step.

3- Gather Required Documents

After submitting the form, you have to gather all the necessary documents that are required for admission into any program at ITI. These documents prove your Genuity.

4- Submit Documents Online

Once you have collected all the required documents as mentioned in the form and/or otherwise demanded by the authorities. You have to upload fresh scans on the website.

5- Choose the TRADE

Competition is always there when it comes to trades. At ITI you will be given a choice of trades according to your merit and you have to choose one.

6- Submit the Fee

After completing all the admission formalities, the last thing to do is submit the fee. You can do it in various ways.
