About Us

Since our inception in 1997, Industrial Training Institutes and Industrial Training Centers are post-secondary schools in India constituted under Directorate General of Training, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Union Government to provide training in various trades


ITI is a good job oriented Technical course; an ITI holder can get a good job in electrical, mechanical, and other manufacturing sectors. So many students are getting Jobs in railways nowadays after ITI.
